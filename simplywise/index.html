Navigation bar

Navigation bar

I introduced a bottom navigation because the current experience feels scattered. Important destinations (Inbox, Reports, Settings) are not consistently one-tap away, and the app relies too much on hidden navigation patterns. My goal here was predictability, not novelty.

What existed (problem)

What existed (problem)

No clear, persistent navigation model, key areas are harder to discover.

What I changed

Why it improves usability

Why it improves usability

Keeps the primary action (capture) consistently available.

Users benefit from predictable tab navigation more than gesture-driven discovery.

Tabs add UI chrome, so the rest of the screens must stay visually disciplined.
