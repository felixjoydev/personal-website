First, a small confession.

SuperGoal is an independent app I built myself. I haven't put it through Apple's notarization yet, so the very first time you open it, macOS will give you a little scare. Here is all you have to do:

  1. You'll see a warning offering Move to Bin or Cancel. Click Cancel, and please don't move it to the bin.
  2. Open System Settings, go to Privacy & Security, scroll to the bottom, and click Open Anyway. Confirm once, and SuperGoal opens for good.

That warning isn't anything to worry about. Notarization is a paid Apple step I simply haven't set up yet as a solo indie developer, so macOS flags the app only because it hasn't seen it before. There is nothing hidden inside. It's a safe little thing, made purely for football fans, and it asks for nothing it doesn't need.

Thank you for taking a chance on a small app. It honestly means the world.
